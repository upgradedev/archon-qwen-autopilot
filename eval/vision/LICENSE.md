# Synthetic fixture provenance

Every file under `eval/vision/assets/` is generated from
`eval/vision/manifest.json` by `generate_fixtures.py`. Names, identifiers,
amounts, layouts, geometric marks, and degradation effects are fictional and
original to this repository. No third-party invoice, logo, template, photograph,
or customer data is used.

The generator and resulting fixtures are distributed under this repository's
MIT License. They are benchmark inputs, not valid financial documents.
