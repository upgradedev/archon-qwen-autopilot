## Claim boundary

This is deterministic **offline application-path** stress evidence: Fake-Qwen,
in-memory storage, zero model spend and no external side effects. It exercises
the HTTP server, intake orchestration, validation, proposal creation, human-gate
queue and read paths. It is not a claim of production Qwen inference throughput,
provider quota capacity, database capacity or end-to-end live-service latency.
Those integrations are evidenced separately by the exact-SHA live canaries and
submission smoke.
